Why make a dedicated PDF invoice?

A clean PDF invoice is easier to send by email, save for accounting and share with a client than a plain order screenshot. FacturePro keeps the document simple: the line items decide subtotal, tax and total, while marketplace fees or platform notes remain informational and do not reduce invoice totals.
