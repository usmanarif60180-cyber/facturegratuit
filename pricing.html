Future Stripe switch

          
Purchasing is disabled now. Later, set billingEnabled and stripeEnabled to true, add Stripe price IDs, then wire checkout.

          
{
  "billingEnabled": false,
  "stripeEnabled": false,
  "checkoutMode": "disabled",
  "purchaseButtons": "disabled"
